SANKALP organization has been working in the area of inclusive Education for Specially Abled and in Rural Development, 4 overlast 13 years(since 2007).



"SANKALPA" has been working for economically marginalized people with Disabilities. And build capacity of different stackholders including ASHA, Anganwadi workers and other front line workers. "SANKALPA" has provided various community services for Persons with Disabilities from rural and urban areas marginalized person's as a part of its strategy "SANKALPA" organized the community and families, it has worked over the years and formed groups for landless, marginalized and vulnerable.



SANKALPA works in close coordination with the distric Disabilityt Rehabilitation Progarm in Davangere. As a part of it we work with different government agencies including the Education Department, Health, Department of women and child development, urban local bodies, rural development & panchyat Raj instituations and Composite Regional Center. Besides the Govt agencies, SANKALPA Works with DPOs, Parents groups on identification assesment, referral, capacity building and implementation of rehabilitation interventions Persons with Disabilities.



SANKALPA enables linking facilities & encouraging the systems to make much-needed provisios for people with disability. We are able to touch lives of 25,000 Persons with Disabilities. We have enabled Persons with Disabilitiesto receive various social security services including the provision of pension, scholarship, health insurance (Niramaya), registration for Unique Disability Identity Cards(UDID), Provision of mobility aids. view more...
